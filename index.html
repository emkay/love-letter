The Begining

Traveling the World

The Wedding

Then we got married. This was the best day ever. I was so happy to find the person to spend the rest of my life with.

The House

Then we bought a house together. It was pretty scary, but in an exciting way.

The Cats

The house was pretty empty so we decided it needed some feline friends. We also bought a new coffee maker.

Then you got pregnant. Again it was both exciting and scary. We went to doctor appointments together. They showed us pictures of our baby. We listened to his heart beat.

The Rescue

We found out that Glory needed a home. What's one more cat anyways?

Alex P. Matuzak

Then our baby boy was born. It was the best day of our lives. We both cried, but it was a good cry.

Making This

I wanted to make this to tell you how much I love you, and how much you impact the most important events in my life. When life becomes busy and hectic sometimes we forget to do the little things that made us fall in love with each other in the first place. This is my way of saying thank you, for not letting me forget. I love you so much, and I never want you to forget it. All my goals in life mean nothing if you aren't happy. I've made a list of things we can try to do more of. It's not perfect or finished, but I think it's a start.

  • Write you a love letter
  • Take you to the movies
  • Take you out to dinner
  • Have a picnic together
  • Take you to the Getty
  • Take you to see a play
  • Take Alex and you to the park
  • Take a family walk together
  • Meditate together
  • Read together
  • Play with the kitties together
  • Sexy time
  • Take a swim

The Future

Keep loving each other!
